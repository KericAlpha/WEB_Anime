General:

The real title of the anime is "Seishun Buta Yaro wa Bani Garu Senpai no Yume o Minai" in english translated "Rascal Does Not Dream Of Bunny Girl Senpai". The anime adaption got released in 2018 and it has 13 episodes and 1 season. The light nover got released in 2014 and the manga in 2015. The studio CloverWorks produced the anime under the direction of Soichi Masui. The anime also has a movie, which is called "Rascal Does Not Dream of a Dreaming Girl". Genres are Comedy, Romance and Slice of Life

Plot:

Sakuta Azusagawa is a high school student and an outsider at his school since he was held responsible for hospitalizing three classmates. His life changes when he sees the teenage actress Mai Sakurajima, who is taking a break from acting, wandering around the library in a rabbit costume and nobody seems to notice her. Mai is fascinated that Sakuta is the only one who can perceive her presence. Sakuta calls this phenomenon "puberty syndrome" and decides to clear up this mystery. He tries to build a relationship with Mai and meets other girls who suffer from the syndrome.

Characters:

Sakuta Azusagawa is a high school student and an outsider. One day when he meets his classmate Sakurajima Mai in a library with a rabbit costume, he decides to investigate the "puberty syndrome". His younger sister's puberty syndrome has also affected him and left him with huge scars. While he investigates about the mystery of the puberty syndrome, Sakuta encounters more victims of the syndrome.



Mai Sakurajima is a young actress and makes a pause, because of the pressure she gets. She attends the same school like Sakuta. She noticed, that people around her forget her existence. To try this, she walks around with a bunny costume. As time goes by more people are forgetting her existence except for Sakuta.




Tomoe Koga attends the same school like Sakuta. She and Sakuta are good friends. As they slowly get closer...







Rio Futaba is the only member of the chemistry club in the school and one of Sakutas friends. Rio didn't believe in the syndrome until she got it herself.






Nodoka Toyohama is Mai Sakurajimas half sister and a member of an idol group. Because of the inferiority complex she and Mai swap their bodies.






Kaede Azusagawa is Sakutas little sister. She suffers from the puberty syndrome, after she got cyber bullied. She cured her sydrome, by not using any sociel media and not going to school. Now, she lives in Sakutas apartment and has anxiety, when she meets strangers.
