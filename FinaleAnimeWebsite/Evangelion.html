General:

Neon Genesis Evangelion (Japanese 新 世紀 エ ヴ ァ ン ゲ リ オ ン Shin Seiki Evangerion, also NGE or EVA for short) is an internationally successful anime television series from 1995. It forms the basis for several later films, mangas and video games and is considered one of the most important to date Anime productions. The work refers to many older films, series and books and had a great influence on subsequent productions. NGE opened up new audiences for the anime medium in Japan and internationally.

Plot:

The series is about the fight against creatures of unknown origin called angels who attack humanity. They are opposed to fighting machines controlled by young people, the Evangelions. As the action progresses, the characters' psychological problems are increasingly addressed. The anime falls into the genres of science fiction, action and mecha and contains various elements from philosophy and psychology.
