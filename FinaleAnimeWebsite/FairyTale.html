General:

My Hero Academia (Japanese 僕 の ヒ ー ロ ー ア カ デ ミ ア, Boku no Hīrō Akademia) is a manga series by Kōhei Horikoshi. It has been appearing in Japan since 2014 and received several spin-off manga as well as an anime television series. The work, which has been translated into several languages, falls into the genres of Shōnen, Drama and Action.

Plot:

Most of the people in the world are born with super powers. Everyone receives a different power and the powers are used for good and bad, so that there is constant fighting between superheroes and super villains. The heroes, whose skills are called “quirks” (個性, kosei, in the anime in German: “specialties”), are trained in special schools. Middle school student Izuku Midoriya (緑谷 出 久) would also like to become a superhero, but he belongs to the 20% of people without such skills. This is where his competitor Katsuki, his girlfriend Ochako and the class representative Tenya appear. The son of the hero Endeavor, Shōto, only uses his ice attacks (he also has fire attacks), but becomes another competitor of Izuku. But Tenya's brother is badly wounded in a fight with Stain, the murderer of the hero, and Izuku and Shōto rush to the aid of their friend.
