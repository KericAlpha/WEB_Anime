General:

The whole title is "Kono Subarashii Sekai ni Shukufuku o!". The anime adaption got released in 2016-2017 and has 20 episodes 2 OVAs in 2 seasons. The light novel got released in 2014 and ended 2020. The anime itsself is not finished yet, so it will get 1 more season for sure. There also is an anime movie called "Kono Subarashii Sekai ni Shukufuku o!: Kurenai Densetsu". Genres are Comedy, Fantasy, Adventure and Isekai.

Plot:

When Kazuma Sato leaves his house to buy a new game, he saved a girl from being run over, but dies. In the afterlife he meets the goddess Aqua, who cannot stop laughing because of the death. Kazuma is now sent to another world and is allowed to take a great item that aqua offered him. He, however, took aqua with him because he was provoked. In order to return from this fantasy world to the real world, Kazuma must defeat the demon king.

Characters:

Kazuma Sato is the main protagonist in this anime. He was sent to a fantasy world with Aqua. Later he met Megumin and Darkness. He has high luck stats and with his party of him, Aqua, Megumin and Darkness, they experience funny and exciting stories.




Before Aqua was sent to the fantasy world with Kazuma, she was a goddess of water who guided humans to the afterlife. In the fantasy world, her stats are pretty high, but she can't use her skills properly.






Megumin is an Arch Wizard of the Crimson Demon Clan and is the first person to join Kazuma's party. The only ability she can use is EXPLOSION!!!. After she used it, she can't move and is vulnerable.






Dustiness Ford Lalatina, usually known as Darkness. She is a crusader with masachistic tendencies who joins Kazuma's party. She is strong, but she misses every single attack.
