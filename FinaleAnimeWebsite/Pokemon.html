General:

Pokemon is well known everywhere. It has many games and animes. It has around 1100 episodes in 23 seasons. The first broadcast was in 1997 in Japan. Pokemon also has many movies. Genres are adventure, comedy, Shonen and action

Plot:

Ash Ketchum, a pokemon trainer, tries to get the champ of the pokemon liga with pikachu and his other pokemons. Pikachu is Ash's best friend and they have a really good relationship.

Characters:

Ash Ketchum is a pokemon trainer, who tries to get the champ of the pokemon liga. In every new region, he catches new pokemons, but Pikachu stays with him.






Misty is a water pokemon trainer. That means she mostly catch and use water pokemons. She accompanies ash through 5 seasons. She grew up in Azuria City with her three sisters and runs a water pokemon arena.






Rocko is a good friend of Ash. He ran a rock pokemon arena and only had rock pokemons, but because of an incident, he stopped to run the arena. Now, he is a cook and pays attention to the nature. Rocko accompanied Ash through many seasons, but now he works as a pokemon doctor.





Gary Eich is Professor Eich's grandson. He and Ash are rivals. He is the champ of the pokemon liga in the Kanto region.






Professor Eich is the expert in the pokemon world. He is a pokemon researcher. He lives in Alabastia, where Ash grew up. In the laboratory he takes care of Ash's pokemon.
