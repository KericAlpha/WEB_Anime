General:

Sword Art Online began as a light novel, then as a manga and then it got an anime adaption. The first broadcast was in 2012. The anime has 96 episodes in 3 seasons. It has 2 movies.

Plot:

Akihiko Kayaba made the NerveGear, a helmet where you can play games in VR. In a game with the NerveGear, you can feel anything, for example you eat something and you really can taste it. When Sword Art Online, a VRMMORPG, started with around 10,000 players they got held captive in the game world. When players die in the game world, they also die in the real world. Even if you take the NerveGear of, you die. Now Kirito, Asuna and many other members of the guild Knight of the Blood Oath tries to defeat the last boss to escape from the death game.

Characters:

Kirigaya Kazuto, better known as Kirito, is the black swordsman in the world of Sword Art Online and is the one, who saved many players from the death game. In season 2 he stopped the series of murder in Gun Gale Online, a VRMMORPG where guns are used as weapons. In season 3 he went with a machine into a fantasy world. He mets Eugeo and they became good friends. Both of them got swordsman and wanted to free a girl called Alice, who got arrested because she broke the Taboo Index.

Yuuki Asuna, better known as Asuna, was called The Lightning in Sword Art Online, because she was really fast. She and Kirito got a pair in the first season and are still one. She uses a rapier, a thin long sword, as her weapon. In season 3 she got into the world Alicization, where she helped Kirito and Alice.




Kirigaya Suguha, better known as Leafa, is Kiritos younger sister. In season 1 she helped Kirito to free Asuna and many other, who got hold captive in Alfheim Online, another VRMMORPG. In season 3 she also got into the world Alicization, where she helped Kirito and Alice.




Asada Shino, better known as Sinon, is a player in Gun Gale Online. Because of an accident, she can't see weapons, but with Gun Gale Online, a VRMMORPG shooter game, she tries to overcome this fear. In season 3 she fought against Dark God Vecta.




Eugeo is a character in the Alicization world. He and Kirito are best friends. They try to free Alice from the Central Cathedral.






Alice is a character in the Alicization world. She got arrested, because she entered the dark territory. As a Integrity Knight, called Alice Synthesis Thirty, she has to protect the human world, if the dark territory is attacking.
