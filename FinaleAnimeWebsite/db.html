General:

Dragon Ball Super (Japanese ド ラ ゴ ン ボ ー ル 超 ス ー パ ー, Doragon Bōru Sūpā) is an anime series produced by Tōei Animation. The first episode aired on July 5, 2015. It is the direct successor to Dragon Ball Z. The action takes place in the ten-year gap between the victory over Boo and the tournament in which Son Goku meets Boo's rebirth Oob. The plot begins four years after Boo was defeated.

Plot:

Four years after Son Goku and his friends defeated Boo, 778 by the Dragon Ball era, Beerus, the god of destruction, awakens from decades of sleep. In his dreams he had a vision of a super Saiyan god who should be an equal opponent. Once there was a super Saiyan god by the name of Yamoshi who wanted to fight the evil Saiyajin. However, it failed. Together with his teacher Whis he goes in search of this super Saiyan god and finally tracks down the last remaining Saiyans Vegeta and Son Goku. He happened to end up at Bulma's birthday party.

Characters:

Son Goku is born as the second son of Bardock and the younger brother of Raditz. He belongs to the Saiyajin people, is given the name Kakarott and is sent to earth by them as a baby to exterminate the people so that the Saiyajin can sell the planet. Son Goku never carries out his assignment, however, as Son Gohan found him shortly after landing, who adopted him and took him in. [1] [2]





Vegeta is a Saiyan like Son Goku. As the son of King Vegeta, he is her prince. He has served Freezer since he destroyed the planet Vegeta and its inhabitants. Raditz and Nappa are his followers. They raid planets (in Freezer's name), wipe out the population and auction him off to the highest bidder. He does this because he knows that he is still too weak to defeat Freezer. So he is waiting to punish him one day for making the "prince of the Saiyajins" (as he often points out) a slave.




Goku Black (ゴ ク ウ ブ ラ ッ ク, Gokū Bukkaku literally "Black Goku"), commonly referred to as Black ("Black"), is the main antagonist of the Trunks (Alternative Future) saga of Dragon Ball Super.







Son Gohan ist ein sehr friedlicher Charakter in der Serie. Anders als sein Vater Son Goku wendet er nur ungern Gewalt an. Mit seinem Trainer Piccolo ist er sehr gut befreundet.








Beerus (ビ ル ス Birusu) is the God of Destruction of Universe 7. He is accompanied by his martial arts teacher and attendant, Whis. Beerus' twin brother is Champa, the God of Destruction of Universe 6.








Whis (ウイス Uisu) is the Guide Angel Attendant of Universe 7's God of Destruction, Beerus,[7] as well as his martial arts teacher. Along with the other angels, he is a child of the Grand Minister. Like all attendants, he is bound to the service of his deity and usually does not leave Beerus unaccompanied.
