Age groups:

There are 5 different age groups for animes:

  • Kodomo ("child"): anime for younger children

  • Shonen ("boy"): anime for male adolescents, often genres like action, science-fiction or fantasy

  • Shojo ("girl"): anime for female adolescents, often love stories

  • Seinen ("young man"): anime for men aged 18 to 30 years, often demanding erotic or violent content

  • Josei ("young woman"): anime for women aged 18 to 30, often everyday or professional and love life of young women

Genre:

There are genres like comedy, action and so on, but anime made some, that can only be used in animes like:

  • Etchi/Ecchi: animes, where sexual indications are happening

  • Gourmet: stories about cooking and eating, it which gourments, restaurant critics or chefs are the main focus

  • Harem: the main protagonist is surrounded the other gender, all of whom are very fond of him/her

  • Hentai: animes, with erotic and perverse content

  • Isekai: the protagonist is sent from the real world into a magical/fantasy world, where he most likely gets superpowers

  • Magical Girl: stories about girls, who can turn into magical warriors and fight monsters

  • Mecha: anime, where robots and machine are the main focus. They are often controlled from within and fight against evil

  • Sentai/Super Sentai: is about a team of superheroes

  • Sport: stories, in which sport is the main focus. The development of the protagonist is followed from the beginning to the professional career

  • Yaoi: an romance anime, where the lovers are boys

  • Yuri: an romance anime, where the lovers are girls
